# 🍫 Chocolate Sales & Market Analysis Dashboard

##  Project Overview
This project is a comprehensive data analysis of a global chocolate retail dataset. Using Excel, I transformed raw transactional data into a multi-layered dashboard to evaluate sales performance, consumer habits, and regional growth.

##  Key Analysis & Sheets
The workbook is organized into specialized sheets to answer specific business questions:

* **Market Penetration:** Analyzed how effectively our products have entered different geographic markets, identifying high-growth vs. stagnant regions.
* **Regional Sales Distribution:** A breakdown of total revenue across the 5 primary countries (USA, India, UK, Canada, and Australia).
* **Regional Taste Preferences:** Used Pivot Tables to map product categories to specific regional trends, helping to understand which chocolates perform best in which cultures.
* **Top 10 Products:** A focused list of the highest revenue-generating products to assist with inventory and marketing priority.

## Technical Implementation
* **Pivot Tables:** Used for complex data aggregation and calculating "% of Total" for taste preference analysis.
* **Dynamic Dashboard:** Integrated Slicers and Charts to allow users to filter data by Country and Product in real-time.
* **Data Cleaning:** Handled date formatting and grouped time-series data into months/years for trend visualization.
* **Logic:** Implemented revenue-per-unit calculations to differentiate between high-volume and high-profit sales.

##  Business Insights
1.  **Top Performance:** The analysis confirms a strong preference for specific product lines in the USA market, while Australia shows higher niche efficiency.
2.  **Seasonality:** Clear revenue peaks were identified in early Q1, suggesting seasonal gifting trends.
3.  **Product Optimization:** The Top 10 list shows that a small percentage of products drive the majority of the profit (Pareto Principle).
